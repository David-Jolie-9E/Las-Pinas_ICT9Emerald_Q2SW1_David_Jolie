MY FAVORITE SERIES CHARACTERS


CHARACTER INFORMATION

Sheldon Cooper

 A child genius who lives in East Texas and wants to become a future scientist.

Missy Cooper

 Twin sister of Sheldon. Not as smart as him but is good at communication.

Georgie Cooper

 Sheldon and Missy's older brother. Doesn't care too much about school and drops out of high school soon later.

George Cooper

 The father. Is a football coach in Medford Highshool and loves eating brisket.

Mary Cooper

 The mother. Very protective over Sheldon and is very religious.

Connie Tucker

 Also known as "Meemaw". The grandmother of Sheldon and his siblings. Likes gambling and drinking beer.
